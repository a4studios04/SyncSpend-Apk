## ✨ Features

* **Apple-Grade Dashboard:** Dynamic, tactile charts that auto-scale to your data. View by Week, Month, or Year without clutter.
* **Privacy First (100% Offline):** Powered by an ultra-fast local **Isar Database**. Your financial data never leaves your phone. No cloud, no trackers, no accounts.
* **Frictionless Quick-Log:** Log an expense in under 2 seconds. Tap, type, and swipe down with a satisfying native fluid popup.
* **Smart Account Separation:** Seamlessly tag expenses under "Personal", "Work", or "A4Studios" to keep your business and personal wallets distinct.
* **Data Portability:** Full support for CSV Export (Backups) and Import (Restores). You own your data.
* **Fluid UI/UX:** Glassmorphism, haptic feedback on every interaction, and native-level vector graphics (including a custom-engineered Rupee symbol).
* **Dark & Light Mode:** Flawless, system-aware dynamic theming.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

* **Framework:** [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/)
* **Language:** Dart
* **Local Database:** [Isar](https://isar.dev/) (NoSQL, ultra-fast)
* **State Management & UI:** Ephemeral state (`setState`), Custom Paint, `fl_chart`
* **Build Targets:** Android (SDK 34)
